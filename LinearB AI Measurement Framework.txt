Establish a methodology:

Measure throughput:
PRs Merged (Merge Frequency)
Time to Merge (TTM)
Measure quality:
PR Maturity
Rework Rate

Internal surveys continue to capture sentiment
gitStream prompts can surface developer feedback within individual PRs, to track edge cases for closer study
